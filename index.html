About Us

Established in 2004 in the village of Badduwa Nanoti, Sangipur, Pratapgarh, Savitri Shiksha Niketan Intermediate College was founded with the mission to provide quality and modern education to children from all sections of society. From its early years as a primary school, it has continuously expanded its reach and impact in the field of education.

Today, the school successfully operates up to Class 8 and aims to extend further. Under the leadership of Principal Shri Vijay Kumar Pandey, the institution believes that education is a form of service, not a business. This guiding philosophy drives our commitment to accessible, inclusive, and holistic education for all.
